MEET THE PLANTS


Discover the native plants that thrive in our garden, each playing a vital role in supporting local wildlife and preserving San Diego's unique biodiversity. From vibrant wildflowers to resilient shrubs, our garden showcases a diverse array of species that are perfectly adapted to the region's climate and ecosystem. Explore the beauty and ecological importance of these native plants as you stroll through our garden, learning about their contributions to habitat restoration and conservation efforts.
