ABOUT

Hi! This is Surabhi. I am an 11th grader at Ranch Bernardo High School, and a Girl Scout Ambassador from troop 2948.

This native plant garden is a Gold Award project led by me. I am working with the Rangers of the Los Penasquitos Preserve as well as members of the community who come to volunteer to make this garden a reality. The site is still under development and has been projected to go until July. If you would like to be a part of this ongoing effort, your contribution would be greatly appreciated. Check out the Get involved tab to learn more!


Project lead by Girl Scout Ambassador, Surabhi Mylar, Troop 2948
